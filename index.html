I am a PhD student at McGill University and a Graduate Research Assistant at Mila - Quebec AI Institute, advised by Siva Reddy. My research aims at developing and improving knowledge-grounded conversational agents. I am currently working on conversational information retrieval for information-seeking dialogs.

My research journey started at Data Analytics and Intelligence Research (DAIR) Group, IIT Delhi where I was advised by Mausam. Before that I was a software developer at Samsung Research Institute Bangalore. I hold a Bachelor of Technology in Mathematics and Computer Science from IIT Guwahati (2013-17).

When I’m not behind a laptop, I like to spend my time outdoors biking, ice-skating, or exploring various events and cultural attractions in Montreal.
